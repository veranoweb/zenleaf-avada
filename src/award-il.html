Frequently Asked Questions

How can I check how many points I have?

Your Rewards Wallet will be your new best friend. Follow the link to enter your phone number and, for easiest access, add your Rewards Wallet link to your home screen like an app.

What are loyalty points in a dispensary?

 Rewards or loyalty points are earned with each purchase. With every dollar spent, you earn 1 point. These points add up with each visit and can be redeemed for exclusive discounts.

Do dispensaries keep track of purchases?

 Dispensaries do keep track of your Rewards points to ensure you receive the discounts earned. This is tracked through the Rewards app, your best tool to ensure you earn rewards points with each visit. You begin earning points after signing up and completing your first purchase.

How do cannabis rewards programs work?

The Rewards program allows you to earn discounts exclusive to Rewards members. Each dollar spent is equal to one Reward point, with points levels unlocking savings:

  • 150 points = $5 OFF
  • 450 points = $25 OFF
  • 700 points = $60 OFF

You can redeem points for Rewards as you meet the point threshold or save them up for the next level of discount.

How fast do points accumulate?

Points accumulate the more you visit Zen Leaf Illinois! Points are tied to your purchase history, so if you purchase a $50 eighth each week, you earn 50 points each week!

When can I start redeeming rewards?

You begin earning points after signing up for the Rewards program and making your first purchase. Rewards can be redeemed once you hit the point level thresholds:

  • 150 points = $5 OFF
  • 450 points = $25 OFF
  • 700 points = $60 OFF

How does redeeming Rewards points work?

Redeeming points is as easy as tapping buttons in your Wallet - at your time of purchase, you simply open your app and access the coupon code! Your Reward will be applied to your cart total first, with any additional in-store discounts applied to the remaining cart balance.

Do points expire?

Points do expire 180 days after earning, if they are not used.

What if I want to opt out of the Illinois Cannabis Rewards Program?

If you want to opt out of the Rewards program, let your Cannabis Advisor know. They will be able to assist in your being removed from Rewards.
